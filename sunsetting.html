./sunsetting-sleepyheads.sh

Sleepyheads was the first project I ever made for the internet. I made it in lockdown for my mum so she could get to sleep easier at night, as she was finding it hard, and there were no good apps available for it at the time. I haven't updated it in years, as I have had other things to work on (and my mum is fine without it now), and I have lost interest in working on it. Here's what you need to know if you still use Sleepyheads:

  • The app and site will be left online for those few who still use it.
  • All pages of the app will include a link to this page.
  • If the app breaks at any point, unless I get some public outcry, it won't be fixed.

I apologise for the inconvenience if you still use the app, but it isn't a project I want to work on anymore. If you want to find out what I do now, visit my website here.

Thanks for using Sleepyheads!
